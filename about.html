Mission

We produce seeds with excellent germination, purity and vigour, ultimately bringing the smile in every farmer who sows our seeds and create value for our customers.

Vision

Our passion to emerge as a leading partner by choice, to produce quality seeds using sustainable agricultural practices, guiding the farming communities to prosper and care for the environment.

About Cava Seed

Cava seeds is an emerging seed producing Company with over 15 years of seed production experience in different vegetables.The passion and vision of us partners lead to the inception of the Company.Apart from the hybrid seed production, we also have great experience in producing many OP varieties of tomatoes, hot peppers, basil and certain flowers, suitable for production in local weather conditions.

The committed and experienced team are based out of production locations for easy and regular monitoring, at all stages of the crop growth. Our technical expertise and close relationship with the farmer base, enables us to take up small scale production trials to identify suitable hybrids and scale up the volume in the forthcoming seasons. The long-term relationship with the farmers is the result of our constant endeavour to maximise their profits, while minimizing the production costs by the

  • Use of new technologies
  • Recommending optimum usage of fertilizers
  • Judicious use of water
  • Integrated pest management

As we grow In the future, our commitment will the directed to embrace sustainable technologies and help the farmers to become more responsible to the environment and preserve the available natural resources for the future generations to come.
